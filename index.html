Tiger

Tigers are the largest wild cats in the world. Adults can weigh up to 363kg
– that’s about the same as ten ten year olds! – and measure up to 3.3m! :Source.

  • Food: Tigers are carnivores, eating only meat.
  • Speed: Full speed they can reach up to 65km/h!
  • Subspecies: Bengal, South China, Indochinese, Sumatran and Siberian
  • Water: Unlike most members of the cat family, tigers like water.

Tigers are solitary hunters, and generally search for food alone at night.
They quietly stalk their prey until they are close enough to pounce –
then they kill their victim with a bite to the neck or back of the head. Ouch!
